Key points on safeguarding policy and fire training

Safeguarding policy key points
Ensure a safe environment for all individuals, especially vulnerable groups
Be aware of signs of abuse
Report if there is any abuse as soon as possible
Understand how to report safeguarding concerns effectively
Be a voice to others if needed
Fire training key points
Being cautious and aware of potential fire hazards in your environment.
Always prioritize personal safety over property.
Follow the emergency procedures
Not leaving any heat unattended
Use fire extinguishers from a distance
Reference:AkiraChix safeguarding policy
